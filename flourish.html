Today's Scripture

"Have I not commanded you? Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged, for the LORD your God will be with you wherever you go."

— Joshua 1:9

Courage in God's Presence

Breakdown

God spoke these words to Joshua as he faced the daunting task of leading Israel into the Promised Land after Moses' death. The command to be courageous is repeated three times in this chapter — clearly, Joshua needed the encouragement, and so do we.

Notice that courage here is not the absence of fear but obedience despite fear. God doesn't say, "Don't feel afraid." He says, "Don't let fear stop you."

The foundation for courage is God's presence: "The LORD your God will be with you wherever you go."

Application

  1. 1 Identify one thing you've been avoiding out of fear — take a small step toward it today.
  2. 2 When fear arises, speak this truth aloud: "God is with me right here, right now."
  3. 3 Memorize Joshua 1:9 and meditate on it before facing any intimidating situation.

Prayer

Mighty God, give me courage to face the giants in my life. When fear whispers that I'm alone, remind me of Your constant presence. Help me step forward in faith, knowing You go before me. In Jesus' name, Amen.
