01

The Root System: Spiritual Foundation

Inductive study, exegesis vs. eisegesis, Armor of God as cognitive framework

Health Spiritual Dimension

Inductive Bible study methodology: observation, interpretation, application. Exegesis vs. eisegesis: drawing meaning OUT of the text, not projecting assumptions IN. The Armor of God operationalized — the belt of truth anchoring identity in Christ, the breastplate of righteousness guarding conscience, the shield of faith extinguishing anxiety, the helmet of salvation protecting hope, the sword of the Spirit as both offensive weapon and defensive recall system.

Wealth Cross-Pillar: Stewardship Alignment

Misreading Scripture equals misaligned priorities. The man who cannot exegete chases mammon believing he is pursuing blessing. Matthew 6:19 is about heart-location, not bank accounts. Exegesis protects against every prosperity-gospel distortion.

Relationships Leadership Foundation

A man who cannot read the Word cannot lead his family. The Armor of God applied to covenant leadership: truth against deception, righteousness against compromise, faith against anxiety, hope against despair. Every piece is needed for every marriage and every child.

Key Scriptures: 1 Cor 10:31; 2 Tim 2:15; Eph 6:10-18
02

Mental Fortitude: Renewing the Mind

Stoicism meets Scripture. Frankl's logotherapy. C.S. Lewis on imagination and reason.

Health Mental Dimension

Solomon meets Epictetus. Emotional regulation from Scripture vs. secular stoicism. Viktor Frankl's "why" as Colossians 3:23 — work as worship, suffering as sanctification. C.S. Lewis sharpens biblical worldview formation through reason and imagination.

Wealth Cross-Pillar: Rational Stewardship

Fear-driven capital allocation vs. mind-renewed stewardship. Romans 12:2 enables rational capital deployment. Anxiety produces hoarding; the renewed mind produces the shrewd manager of Matthew 25.

Relationships Non-Reactive Leadership

Emotional regulation is prerequisite for non-reactive leadership. A man who does not control his own spirit cannot lead his household. The stoic-integrated Christian brings peace under pressure.

Key Scriptures: Rom 12:2; Phil 4:7; Col 3:23
03

Temple Building: Physical Stewardship

Health Physical Dimension

Resistance training, fasting, nutrition, sleep architecture. The body as temple — not vanity but vocational preparation. Elijah fled Jezebel in exhaustion; after physical restoration, he heard God's still small voice.

Key Scriptures: 1 Cor 6:19-20; 1 Kings 19; 1 Tim 4:8
04

Breaking Chains: Debt Elimination

Wealth Consumer Credit Theology

Proverbs 22:7: the borrower is slave to the lender. Debt elimination as spiritual liberation. Snowball method. Covenant financial unity as marital restoration.

Key Scriptures: Prov 22:7; Rom 13:8; Ps 37:21
05

Earned Income: Work as Worship

Wealth Vocational Mastery

Colossians 3:23 in the cubicle. Work as worship, not merely a means to a paycheck. Career as calling. The 4-Week Types of Income intensive begins here.

Key Scriptures: Col 3:23-24; 1 Tim 5:8; Ecc 9:10
06

Multiple Streams: Portfolio Income

Wealth Diversification

Dividends, interest, capital gains. Money working while you sleep. Ecclesiastes 11:1-2: diversify across waters. The single-income family is financially fragile.

07

Generational Allocation: Kingdom Legacy

Wealth Legacy Building

Proverbs 13:22: a good man leaves an inheritance. Not merely money — transmitted values, discipleship, and kingdom alignment across generations.

08

Passive Income: Cash Flow Engineering

Wealth Automated Streams

Real estate, royalties, digital products, automated businesses. Psalm 128:2: you will eat the fruit of your labor. Building systems that produce while you sleep.

09

Compounding: Exponential Kingdom

Wealth The Eighth Wonder

Compounding applied to capital, relationships, and spiritual influence. The man who understands exponential growth understands kingdom multiplication.

10

Covenant Marriage: Romantic Leadership

Relationships Sacrificial Leadership

Ephesians 5:25-33: love as Christ loved the church. Covenant marriage as the single greatest wealth protector and health stabilizer. The man leads by serving.

11

Crisis Resilience: Faith Over Fear

Health Spiritual Warfare

The shield of faith extinguishes anxiety. Recession-proof mindset. Faith over fear as the distinguishing mark of kingdom men in crisis.

12

Accountability & Mission: Sending Disciples

Relationships Commissioning

Radical self-reflection. Integrity audit. Personal accountability structures. The graduate of Flourish becomes a discipler, not merely a disciple.

Database Architecture

Interactive Schema

PostgreSQL. Soft deletes, foreign keys, RBAC. Hover tables to see relationships glow. All fields from the PRD included.

Security Architecture

TLS 1.3, AES-256 at rest, column-level encryption for PII, Signal Protocol for pastoral counseling, RBAC with 6 role tiers, GDPR/CCPA compliant 30-day grace + 7yr archive.

Tech Stack

React Native (Expo) / Flutter → NestJS + Socket.io → PostgreSQL + Redis + BullMQ → Stripe Connect → OneSignal → Mapbox.

Soft Deletes

Every table has created_at, updated_at, deleted_at. Hard deletion triggers 30-day grace → pseudonymization → 7yr encrypted cold archive for tax retention.
