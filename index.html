Day 127 of 365

Joshua 1:9

Scripture

"Have I not commanded you? Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged, for the LORD your God will be with you wherever you go."

— Joshua 1:9 NIV

Breakdown

God commands strength and courage—not suggests, commands. Fear and discouragement are not options for those who carry divine presence. "Wherever you go" means geographic, emotional, and spiritual territory. The covenant promise is mobile.

Application

1

Health: Start your morning with 10 minutes of prayer before checking your phone. Physical discipline trains spiritual discipline.

2

Wealth: Review your budget today with courage. Face the numbers without fear—God is with you in financial decisions too.

3

Relationships: Have one courageous conversation you've been avoiding. Speak truth with love, knowing God goes before you.

Prayer
